I hold a postgraduate certificate (PGCert) in [Teaching and Learning in Higher Education from the University of Oxford](https://www.ctl.ox.ac.uk) and am a Fellow of the [Higher Education Academy](http://www.heacademy.ac.uk). 

I currently teach three undergraduate courses at Oxford: `Introduction to Politics`, `Political Sociology`, and `Modern British Politics`. 

**Past courses**

I have previously taught a range of courses in comparative politics as lecturer, seminar leader, tutorials and other formats. These include courses in `Political Behaviour`, `Global Governance`, and `Research Methods`.
